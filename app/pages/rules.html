English version

  1. Use your common sense.
  2. Conversations only in Spanish or English (this one preferably in #english).
  3. Respect everyone equally. If you feel victim of harassment, please contact the Staff.
  4. No spam (repeated messages, excessive use of caps, tagging users, mini-modding, etc.). Bot command spam allowed only in #bots. Spotify invitations must go on #music. Invitations to other servers must be approved by the Staff.
  5. Avoid derailing conversations (voice and/or text chat). Use those channels wisely. The staff may ask to move any conversation it deems appropriate if it’s considered off-topic.
  6. Explicit and suggestive content, in addition to gore, is strictly forbidden, due to special rules for Discord Partner servers.
  7. Fraudulent or referral links are forbidden. The Staff may ask for a VirusTotal analysis to accept a link.
  8. In case of any doubt, along with our rules, we follow Discord’s Community Guidelines. They can be read here: https://discord.cl/guidelines.
  9. We reserve the right to admission and to eliminate any content without previous advice, given proper justification.
Any other action deemed annoying or inadequate for the convivence inside the server, could be object of sanction. Rules will be applied depending on the severity of the situation and the staff’s discretion.
